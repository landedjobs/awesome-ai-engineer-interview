> [!NOTE]
> **The loop at a glance.** Recruiter → a **90-minute screening** (LLM theory + coding) → a **3-hour technical assessment** (language modelling + coding) → **ML system design** + **deep dive** + **behaviorals**. A **7-round** loop spanning **4–6 weeks**, leveled IC3–IC6. The distinguishing round is the **3-hour technical assessment** — the deepest single sitting on this list.

## The loop

1. **Recruiter call.**
2. **Screening (90 min)** — topics from language modelling to coding.
3. **Technical assessment (3h)** — language modelling, ML system design, deep dive.
4. **ML system design** (onsite).
5. **Deep dive** (onsite).
6. **Behaviorals** (onsite).

## What they're really testing

- **Deep language-modelling fluency.** The 3-hour assessment is a genuine test of LM understanding, not trivia.
- **Stamina and consistency.** Seven rounds across weeks reward sustained quality, not a single peak.
- **Enterprise-grade system design.** Cohere sells to enterprises — expect tenancy, isolation, and reliability in the design round.
- **Depth over breadth in the deep dive.** They want the hardest problem you actually solved, in detail.
- **Level calibration (IC3–IC6).** The loop is used to place you, so scope your answers to real ownership.
